Our Aim

In the United States alone, about 150,000 pounds of food are thrown away each day. Food waste is a prevalent problem, whether it is due to unclear expiration labels or forgetfulness.

Our web application strives to help college students reduce the amount of food waste tossed. College students have to juggle classes, work, and commuting. By exchanging or donating food with other students, each user can worry less about what they are going to eat each day, and about the money they waste by throwing away food they could not eat in time.

Users can select to Drop Off their food by uploading details about the item, where they would like to drop off the food, and the time they are available. Users who want to pick up food can view items offered for Drop Off that fit their schedule and location.
